I am a historian of the Roman imperial world, currently completing my PhD in the Combined Program in Classics and History at Yale University. My dissertation, entitled "Keeping Time, Constructing Community: Belonging in the Roman Provinces," examines how provincial communities constructed and expressed belonging through shared conceptions of time and participation in temporal rhythms. This project is rooted in my broader interests ethnicity and culture in the Roman provinces.
